Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and was known as the "People's President" and the "Missile Man of India" for his contributions to India's space and missile programs.
